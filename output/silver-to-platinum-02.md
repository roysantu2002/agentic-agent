#### **MODULE 2: Core Identity & Destiny (Pythagorean Deep Dive)**

**Unit 2.1: The Life Path Number**
*   **Topic 2.1.1: Concept & Calculation**
    *   **Content:** The Life Path is the most important number in the chart. It represents the broad outline of your life's journey, your core lessons, challenges, and opportunities. It is unchanging.
    *   **Calculation Method:**
        1.  Write out full birth date in DD/MM/YYYY format.
        2.  Reduce the Day, Month, and Year to single digits or master numbers *individually*.
        3.  Add the three resulting numbers together.
        4.  Reduce that sum to a single digit or master number.
    *   **Example:** December 25, 1985.
        *   Month: 12 -> 1+2 = 3
        *   Day: 25 -> 2+5 = 7
        *   Year: 1985 -> 1+9+8+5 = 23 -> 2+3 = 5
        *   Sum: 3 + 7 + 5 = 15 -> 1+5 = **6**
        *   **Life Path is 6.**
*   **Topic 2.1.2: Interpretation**
    *   **Content:** Detailed breakdown for each Life Path (1-9, 11, 22, 33). For each number, cover:
        *   **Positive Traits:** Natural strengths and abilities.
        *   **Negative Traits:** Potential challenges and shadow aspects.
        *   **Life Lesson:** The core theme they are here to learn.
        *   **Career Paths:** Suitable environments.

**Unit 2.2: The Destiny Number**
*   **Topic 2.2.1: Concept & Calculation**
    *   **Content:** The Destiny (or Expression) Number reveals your natural talents, abilities, and the person you are meant to become. It's "what you are doing here" in terms of your capabilities.
    *   **Calculation Method:**
        1.  Use the **Pythagorean Letter-Number Chart:**
            *   1: A, J, S
            *   2: B, K, T
            *   3: C, L, U
            *   4: D, M, V
            *   5: E, N, W
            *   6: F, O, X
            *   7: G, P, Y
            *   8: H, Q, Z
            *   9: I, R
        2.  Write out your full birth name (first, middle, last) as it appears on the birth certificate.
        3.  Convert each letter to a number.
        4.  Add all numbers together.
        5.  Reduce the total to a single digit or master number.
    *   **Example:** John William Doe
        *   J-O-H-N = 1+6+8+5 = 20
        *   W-I-L-L-I-A-M = 5+9+3+3+9+1+4 = 34
        *   D-O-E = 4+6+5 = 15
        *   Total: 20 + 34 + 15 = 69 -> 6+9 = 15 -> 1+5 = **6**
        *   **Destiny Number is 6.**

**Unit 2.3: The Soul Urge & Personality Numbers**
*   **Topic 2.3.1: Soul Urge (Heart's Desire) Number**
    *   **Content:** Your inner motivation, your heart's deepest desire, what truly makes you happy. Calculated from the **vowels** in your full birth name.
    *   **Example:** John William Doe
        *   Vowels: O, I, I, A, O, E = 6+9+9+1+6+5 = 36 -> 3+6 = **9**
*   **Topic 2.3.2: Personality Number**
    *   **Content:** The "gatekeeper," the first impression you make, how others perceive you when they first meet you. Calculated from the **consonants** in your full birth name.
    *   **Example:** John William Doe
        *   Consonants: J,H,N, W,L,L,M, D = 1+8+5+5+3+3+4+4 = 33 -> **33/6**

**Unit 2.4: Supporting Numbers**
*   **Topic 2.4.1: Birthday Number**
    *   **Content:** A specific gift or talent you bring into this life. Simply the day of the month you were born, reduced to a single digit. (e.g., Born on the 25th: 2+5=7).
*   **Topic 2.4.2: Maturity Number**
    *   **Content:** The theme that emerges in the second half of your life (often after age 35-40). Calculated by adding your **Life Path + Destiny Number** and reducing.
    *   **Example:** Life Path 6 + Destiny 6 = 12 -> 1+2 = **3**. The individual's later life will take on a more expressive, creative, and social (3) flavor.
